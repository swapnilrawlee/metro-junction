PRIVACY POLICY

At Metro Junction Mall, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our customers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal information about our customers when they use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information about you when you visit our website, create an account, place an order, participate in a survey or promotion, or otherwise interact with us. The types of personal information we collect may include:

  • Your name, email address, postal address, and phone number
  • Payment information, such as your credit card number and billing address
  • Information about your purchases, including the products you have bought
  • Your preferences and interests, such as the types of products you are interested in
  • Your communication preferences, such as whether you prefer to receive emails or text messages from us
  • Other information you provide to us, such as when you contact us with a question or comment

We may also collect certain non-personal information about your use of our website, such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To process your orders and provide you with our products and services
  • To communicate with you about your orders, account, or other inquiries
  • To send you promotional emails, newsletters, or other marketing materials that may be of interest to you
  • To improve our website, products, and services
  • To prevent fraud and other illegal activities

We may also use your information for other purposes that are disclosed to you and to which you consent.

Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your personal information to third parties for the following purposes:

  • To our service providers, who perform services on our behalf, such as shipping, payment processing, and marketing
  • To our affiliates, who may use your information for their own marketing purposes
  • To comply with legal obligations, such as a court order or subpoena
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety or those of others

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Security of Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure. However, we cannot guarantee the security of your information, and you should take steps to protect your own information, such as using a strong password and keeping it confidential.

You may choose to opt-out of receiving promotional emails or text
