About

West Pioneer Properties (India) Pvt. Ltd. (WPPIPL) is a leading retail-led mixed use developer and operator of high-quality consumer shopping malls along with residential and commercial developments in India.

The company's pioneering spirit is very evident in its vision of seeing possibilities for future growth and development, and turning them into on-ground success stories. This has seen it zero in on and acquire vast tracts of land in strategic locations in Tier II cities in western and southern parts of the country; the engines of potentially enormous growth in the near future. These land holdings form the basis of developments to cater to the burgeoning demands of a rapidly modernising society. Based on the potential of specific sites and their strategic location advantages, West Pioneer Properties will undertake the development of shopping malls and entertainment hubs, commercial complexes, mall cum hotel complexes as well as premium residential projects. In true pioneering spirit, each development is marked by the foresight of anticipating the needs of the populace well in advance, and making the most of the first-mover's advantage. Having already tasted success with its first few projects, the company is poised to grow from strength to strength.F.
